## Working title

"From silent list to working hive: email automation for Bee-Licious Honey"

## The shape (case-study template v1, reusable)

1. **The problem.** Bee-Licious had a WooCommerce store, a Klaviyo account
   wired but idle: hundreds of past-buyer profiles, zero flows live, a draft
   signup form, and no automated follow-up on carts, signups, or education.
   Every visitor who didn't buy on the spot was gone for good.
2. **What shipped.** Three flows built and taken live 2026-06-23: a 4-email
   Welcome journey (immediate gift email, then honey education on a cadence
   Jen tightened herself), a 3-email LEARN nurture for research-curious
   subscribers (with conditional split on purchase history), and a 2-email
   abandoned-cart recovery. Plus two signup popups (site-wide + LEARN page)
   routing subscribers by `signup_source`, ten hand-built templates through
   eight revision rounds in Jen's voice with her own photography, and a
   written brand voice guide distilled from her nine principles.
3. **The craft detail that sells it.** Voice. Jen's own verdict mid-project,
   quote (pending her permission): "this finally feels like Bee-Licious."
   Templates verified on real phone viewports, all CTAs link-checked live.
4. **Proof it works.** End-to-end verified the day it went live: a real
   signup through the live popup received the welcome gift email about three
   minutes later, confirmed in the recipient inbox. A three-weeks-in
   performance report went to the client 2026-07-15
   (deliverables/Bee-Licious-Performance-Update-2026-07-15.pdf); pull
   published numbers FROM THAT REPORT only after Jen approves which ones.
5. **The next rung (retainer pitch context, internal).** Post-purchase
   thank-you email is built and staged pending Jen's yes. Ongoing flow
   tuning, seasonal sends, and list growth = the natural monthly engagement.
6. **Pull quote candidate:** "this finally feels like Bee-Licious" (Jen,
   2026-06-05 email). Alternative: her go-live "LET'S GO LIVE!" energy,
   paraphrased with permission.
